The energy balance equation is

Energy in - Energy out = (Q in - Q out ) + (W in - W out) + (E mass,In - E mass,out)

To Find the Thermal Efficiency

Mathematically it is represented as;

nth = Wnet / Q in

Where nth is the Thermal Efficiency, The Wnet (in joules or any other consistent unit of energy) is the useful Work Output, And the Q In (in joules or any other consistent unit of energy) is the energy Input as Heat

Efficiency is measured in percentage, so multiply the answer by 100%, or just include it into the fromula as shown below

Nth = (Wnet / Q in) * 100%
